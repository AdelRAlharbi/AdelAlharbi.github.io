BIOGRAPHY

Adel R. Alharbi was born and raised in Tabuk City, Saudi Arabia. He received the Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from Qassim University, Saudi Arabia in 2008, and two Masters of Science degrees in Security Engineering and Computer Engineering from Southern Methodist University, The United States of America, Texas, Dallas in 2013 and 2015 respectively. He also had the degree of Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Engineering from Southern Methodist University, The United States of America, Texas, Dallas in 2017. Adel has been a faculty staff member at College of Computer Science and Information Technology in University of Tabuk, Saudi Arabia since 2009. He acquired several academic certificates and published many scientific papers. He is interested in research involving mobile and smart device security and he is specifically investigating biometric, security, and machine learning techniques for smart device applications.
