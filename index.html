BIOGRAPHY

Adel R. Alharbi was born and raised in Tabuk City, Saudi Arabia. He received the Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from Qassim University, Saudi Arabia in 2008, and two Masters of Science degrees in Security Engineering and Computer Engineering from Southern Methodist University in 2013 and 2015 respectively. He also had the degree of Doctor of Philosophy in Computer Engineering from Southern Methodist University in 2017. He has been a faculty staff member at the University of Tabuk, Saudi Arabia since 2009. Adel is interested in research involving mobile and smart device security and he is specifically investigating biometric security and machine learning techniques for smart device applications.
